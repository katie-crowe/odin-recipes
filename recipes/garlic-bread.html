Garlic Bread

Description

This simple garlic bread recipe is perfect for anyone who has a craving for garlic bread but is short on time. The entire process takes just about half an hour, and you'll have enough garlic bread to share!

Ingredients

  • Demi-baguette
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 teaspoons of salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees.
  2. Make slices along the demi-baguette, about 3/4 inch apart. The slices should almost cut through the bread, but not completely, so that the bread is left intact and connected.
  3. Soften the butter by putting it in the microwave for 5-10 seconds, or as long as it takes to get it soft but not completely melted and liquidy.
  4. Mince the garlic and add to the softened butter.
  5. Add the salt.
  6. Once the oven is preheated, place the demi-baguette onto a sheet of foil and close the foil over the demi-baguette so that it is completely covered. This will ensure that the bread is soft and not crispy.
  7. After 15 minutes, remove the foil-wrapped demi-baguette from the oven, and serve! It should be easily torn apart for sharing.
