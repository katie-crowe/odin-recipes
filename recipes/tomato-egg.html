Tomato Egg

Description

Stir-fried tomato and egg is one of the most comforting Cantonese dishes, and is super easy to make. With just a handful of ingredients, you'll have dinner ready in no time!

Ingredients

  • 3 tomatoes, cut in medium sized pieces, or 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 3 eggs
  • Several slices of fresh ginger
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • Corn starch slurry (corn starch + water mixture), amount to your taste
  • Rock sugar
  • 1/3 cup water
  • A squirt of ketchup (optional)
    •

    Steps

    1. Scramble the eggs in a bowl.
    2. Heat the wok on the highest heat setting.
    3. Once the wok is heated, add the scrambled eggs.
    4. Once the eggs are 80% cooked, remove the eggs from the wok.
    5. Now, heat the wok at medium heat, and add the tomatoes, ginger, rock sugar, and water.
    6. Once the tomatoes are cooked down and the rock sugar has dissolved, add the corn starch slurry, making sure to stir as you add.
    7. Add back the scrambled eggs, stir into the mixture, and turn the heat off.
    8. You can add a squirt of ketchup and mix in to add some tangy sweetness, if you like.
    9. Recommended to serve over white rice.
